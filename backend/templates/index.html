Introduction to medOut

medOut is an application that focuses on a mobile clinic service optimized for the patients needs. We used data stemming from socioeconomic aspects of various neighborhoods in Boston to seasonal factors on numerous preventable diseases to be implemented in a clinic service.

What exactly does it do?

medOut streamlines the patient process at medical clinics through a text chatbot and collects client inforrmation while informing them about upcoming clinics.

Data flexibility

medOut runs a machine learrning algoririthm to best determine locations and care measures for mobile clinics.

Challenges encountered

Some challenges we faced when implementing this application revolves around the data. Essentially, we were analyzing thousands of data points in order to determine an algorithm that would best determine which route the mobile clinic should take to meet the highest ratio of population with necessity at a specific location.
